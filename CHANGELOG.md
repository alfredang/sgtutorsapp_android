## [1.0] — 2026-07-06 · versionCode 1

### Added
- First release of Tertiary SGTutors for Android.
- Search Singapore home tutors by name, subject, level, region and gender.
- Featured tutors carousel and infinite-scroll tutor list.
- Tutor profiles with verified badges, star ratings, experience, students taught, subjects, levels, education background and reviews.
- Send an enquiry directly to a tutor — no account needed.
- Feedback via WhatsApp and About screens.
- Material 3 indigo theme with light and dark mode.
